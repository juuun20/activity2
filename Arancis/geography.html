GEOGRAPHY

Ilocos Sur is characterized by its coastal plains, rolling hills, and mountain ranges. The province is bordered by the Cordillera Central mountain range to the east and the South China Sea to the west. The Abra River, one of the longest rivers in the Philippines, flows through the province.
